About Us

Crossed Swords Guild is an independent community guild made for the Crossed Swords game. Just like the motto "Fighting others fairly while building each other", our guild strive by fighting others, in this case, enemy fairly while building the members up by counting on each other.

Goals

We aim for anyone that takes game as an entertaining, stimulating and most importantly bring happiness. Any ages are welcome!
